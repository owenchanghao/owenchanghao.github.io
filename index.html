Journal

On Guangzhou's City Management (2022-4-18)

The Omicron spike began since April 8th, 2022. Only within 10 days, the infection rate is reduced from 5.8 downto 0.98 without rigorous lockdowns or unecessary enforced self-isolations. Guangzhou got the variant of Covid-19 wave under control within 2 weeks. I won't say it's a shiny example in the epidemiology history, but this is remarkable in itself. Looking at Shanghai now, uncapable to cut community spread and the numbers were above 20,000 per day for at least a fortnight. Today, 3 died from Covid in Shanghai. It's a warning.

To control the pandemic, blah blah blah does not work. Egotism is counterproductive. We need accuracy, cooperation, humility, hands-on and firm resolution humanitarianly, raising city management skills to the next level. I won't say Guangzhou will always successfully control all outbreaks in the future, but the neo-generation epidemiologist talents and municipal management skills are catalysts for a brighter city. Zhong Nanshan blesses Guangzhou.
